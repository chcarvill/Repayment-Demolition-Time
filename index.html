TIP
To get your balance history from your bank statement: log in to your internet banking and download your credit card statement as a CSV file. Open it in Excel or Numbers, then find the Balance or Running Balance column — it's usually the last column on the right. Click the column header to select all the values, then copy and paste directly into the box below. Bank statements typically show the most recent transaction first, so use Plot — first entry is newest. If you're typing your balances in manually from oldest to most recent, use Plot — first entry is oldest.
